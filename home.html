DINER CODE OF CONDUCT IN THIS LIGHT OF COVID-19


Before You Arrive:


• Stay home if you’re sick or experiencing any symptoms of COVID-19.
• Let us know as early as possible if your plans have changed and you need to change your reservation.
• We’re able to guarantee your table for 15 minutes past your reservation time. If you are late more than 15 minutes for your reservation, your table may be given away to another party.

Upon Arrival


Parties may only be seated at their table if:
• All guests in the party have arrived and are wearing appropriate face coverings that cover their mouth and nose.
• All guests in the party have their temperature taken upon entering our restaurant and have a temperature no greater than 37'C.
• One member of each party provides a photo ID along with their full name, home address, and phone number for use in contact tracing.

While Dining


• We’d appreciate it if all guests in a party kept on their masks while ordering and until food or drinks are delivered to the table.
• If you need to grab our attention during your meal, we’d appreciate it if all guests at a table put on their masks before staff approach the table.
• In order to practice physical distancing and prevent crowding near the restroom area, please wait for the restroom at your table. Notify your server to be added to the queue.
